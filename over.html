About De Filosoof

De Filosoof is a periodical magazine published by and for the Department of Philosophy and Religious Studies at Utrecht University. Each issue of our magazine is a balance of (accessible) academic philosophical articles, columns, and literary contributions, such as short stories and poetry. All of this is accompanied by rich illustrations, handcrafted by our illustrators. Our circulation consists of 600 physical copies, which are sent to all faculty and students (BA Philosophy, MA Applied Ethics, RMA Philosophy) of the department. We publish three times per academic year. The magazine was founded by students in the year 2000, and our editorial board still consists of students from the aforementioned department. They are from both bachelor's and master's levels.
